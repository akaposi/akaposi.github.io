Ambrus Kaposi

I'm a lecturer at the Department of Programming Languages and Compilers at Eötvös Loránd University, Budapest, Hungary. I have a bachelors degree in computer science from the same university, and hopefully I will soon have a PhD in type theory from the University of Nottingham where I spent three years in the Functional Programming Laboratory under the supervision of Graham Hutton. Previously I did a PhD on medical statistics after becoming a medical doctor at Semmelweis University.

Email: akaposi@inf.elte.hu
